JRF Regional Scholar Advisory Committees

Pacific Northwest  | Western |  Southwestern  | Central |  Upper Midwest |  Southeastern |  Mid-Atlantic  | Northeastern

There are eight regional advisory committees from around country that assists JRF in screening the initial qualified applications; selecting and interviewing semi-finalists; recommending candidates to be considered by JRF Headquarters as finalists; providing mentoring for Jackie Robinson Scholars; supporting JRF events; participating with local Scholars and alumni; and acting as ambassadors. The committees are comprised of academic, corporate, and civic leaders, many of whom are alumni.
